Our Story

In April 2019, Chef José Esteban Rivero and his wife Sarah Santos opened PROLE Cocina & Barra in the vibrant Calle Cerra neighborhood of Santurce, Puerto Rico.

After Hurricane Maria, José and Sarah returned to Puerto Rico with a mission: to support the local economy and champion Puerto Rican agriculture. With 11 years of culinary training in Philadelphia, José brings international expertise infused with island passion.

Our De la Finca a la Mesa (Farm-to-Table) philosophy isn't just a concept—it's our commitment to the resilience and beauty of Puerto Rico. Every dish tells a story of fresh, seasonal ingredients and the farmers who grow them.

Fresh. Local. Inspired.

Our menu changes every two to three days, reflecting the freshest available ingredients from local Puerto Rican farms. Each visit to PROLE offers a new culinary experience, crafted with creativity and rooted in quality.

Local Partnerships

Every ingredient tells a story. We work directly with Puerto Rican farmers, ensuring the freshest produce while supporting our local agricultural community. This direct relationship allows us to know exactly where our food comes from.

Farm to Table
